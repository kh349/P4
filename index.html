The Quote of the Day!


"Imperfection is beauty, Madness is genius, and it's better to be absolutely ridiculous than absolutely boring"
- Marilyn Monroe

Our goal is to make a community of crafters to share tips and helpful links(connections) to other services that potential DIYs’ would want. Pinterest offers seemingly possible DIY’s while their links and or sources are usually an ad to draw in customers to clicking a site. While blogs are tedious to filter through to find a weekend job that anyone can do. To do this we hope to create a tag system that can help potential DIYers’ to find a fun hobby, new skill or weekend job, as well as, a team of moderators to make sure the site is free from spam or disguised ads.

Kathy Huang, IS117, Spring 2020

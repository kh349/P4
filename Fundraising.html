Our goal is a non-profit website that everyone can use while ads that are moderated can filter through and suggest places that supplies can be purchased.
Ideally, we hope to create a site can be supported by its community and the current system of ads, but at a more controlled rate where the annoyance of ads or useless DIYs are deleted. Visitors can subscribe to certain blogs and donate if they wish to the site as a whole or to a certain user. If there are extra income from donations and ads, we could form a community vote to a charity to donate the extra income or save it for when we need money depending on the amount and the cost of site maintenance from mods. As for mods themselves, they will be vetted by other pre-established mods and given a pay if they wish depending on the work they do.
